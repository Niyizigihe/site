OurCoreActivities

At Ejo Heza Hacu, we engage in various activities aimed at empowering people with disabilities through sustainable employment and skill development. Our programs focus on creating lasting impact and fostering independence.

Skills Training

We provide comprehensive vocational training programs tailored for people with disabilities, focusing on marketable skills that lead to employment or self-employment opportunities.

Handicraft Production

Our members create high-quality handicrafts using traditional and modern techniques, showcasing their creativity while generating sustainable income through local and international markets.

Business Development

We support our members in establishing and growing their own businesses through mentorship, business planning assistance, and connecting them with market opportunities.

Community Outreach

Through awareness campaigns and community engagement, we work to change perceptions about disability and promote inclusion in Rwandan society.

Education Support

We facilitate access to education and training opportunities for people with disabilities, helping them develop the knowledge and skills needed for professional growth.

Advocacy

We advocate for the rights and inclusion of people with disabilities in all aspects of society, working with stakeholders to create more accessible and inclusive environments.

About Us

EJO HEZA HACU Cooperative

EJO HEZA HACU Cooperative is dedicated to empowering people with disabilities in Rwanda by creating sustainable employment opportunities and promoting innovation. Our mission is to uplift individuals with disabilities through meaningful work, enabling them to become self-reliant and valued members of society. Driven by our vision, we advocate for awareness and inclusion, fostering creativity and entrepreneurship to inspire change. We are committed to breaking the cycle of dependency and eliminating the culture of begging, paving the way for dignity, independence, and equal opportunities for all.
